Banaue Rice Terraces

The Rice Terraces are located high up in the mountains of Ifugao. They look like giant green stairs made of land. A long time ago, people built these terraces using only their hands and simple tools. They did this so they could plant rice on the side of the mountain. It is very green everywhere and the air is very cold and fresh. There are also many waterfalls and rivers nearby. It is called the "Eighth Wonder of the World" because it is a very amazing thing that humans made.
