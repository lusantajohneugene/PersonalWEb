Mount Fuji, Japan

Mount Fuji is an iconic active volcano and the cultural symbol of Japan. Located on Honshu Island, it is world-famous for its perfectly symmetrical snow-capped cone and its spiritual significance to the Japanese people.

I want to visit this place because I want to see the highest and largest mountain in Japan. Standing at 3,776 meters, it is a magnificent sight that I want to experience in person to appreciate its massive scale and natural beauty.
