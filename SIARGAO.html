Siargao Island

Siargao is a beautiful island that is famous for its big waves. Many people go there to learn how to surf in the ocean. The island has many thousands of coconut trees and a long road that goes through them. There are also natural pools in the rocks where you can swim when the water is low. The water is very green and blue. The people living there are very friendly and they like to help tourists. It is a very cool and relaxed island where you can enjoy the simple life.

I also want to jump into the Magpupungko rock pools and swim in the clear water. I want to try the healthy food and fruits that grow on the island.
