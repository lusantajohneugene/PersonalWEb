Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai is a city of superlatives, home to the world's tallest building, the Burj Khalifa, and massive man-made islands. It is a global hub known for its futuristic architecture, luxury shopping, and vibrant nightlife set against a stunning desert backdrop.

I want to visit this place because I want to sky dive over the iconic Palm Jumeirah and explore different locations in the UAE. From the grandeur of Abu Dhabi to the natural mountains of Hatta, I want to experience everything this unique region has to offer.
